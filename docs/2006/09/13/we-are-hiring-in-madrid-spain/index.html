We are hiring in Madrid (Spain)

Sep 13, 2006 in UNCATEGORIZED
1 min read

The new term has started, and we are going to make a new effort to make our company Amplía Soluciones S.L. bigger. So we have just started a new recruiting procress. If you have reached this blog, you will probably have affinity with us. We are a young company that have grown from 2 people in 2004 to 30 in 2006. And we don’t want to stop.

We are looking for:

– Java Developers, with or without experience in J2EE and the de-facto standards of the industry: Hibernate, Spring, Struts…
– C and/or C++ Developers with or without experience developing on embedded devices .
– Project Managers with Strong Technical Background.
– Analysts with experience with UML modelling and J2EE architectures.
– Software Architects with experience in J2EE.
– Software Architects with experience in Embedded devices.
– Testers: Quality Assurance personnel.
– Systems engineers with strong background in Networking and Unix systems: Solaris and Linux is a must.

If you think you can fit in some of our open positions, you can post your resumé here (spanish) or here (english).
